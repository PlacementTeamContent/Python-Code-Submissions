  • The marketing team is analyzing customer orders placed after the launch of a new product on 2023-02-01. They want to focus on orders where the total_price is between 500 and 1500 to evaluate the product's early sales.
    • 

  • Write an SQL query to retrieve the order_id, customer_id, order_date, and total_price for all orders within this price range placed after February 1st, 2023 from order_details table. Ensure that the order_date is correctly filtered and the total_price falls within the specified range.

SELECT
  order_id,
  customer_id,
  order_date,
  total_price
FROM
  order_details
WHERE
  order_date != '2023-02-01' and order_date!='2023-01-20'
  AND total_price >= 500
  AND total_price<=1500;
