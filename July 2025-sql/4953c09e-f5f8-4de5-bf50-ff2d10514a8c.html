2024 Deposit Summary for Financial Reporting

The financial reporting team needs a summary of all deposit transactions for the year 2024 to prepare an annual financial statement. Write an SQL query to find the total amount of deposits made between 2024-01-01 and 2024-12-31.

Sample Output Table:

total_deposits
... 
SELECT
  sum(Amount) AS total_deposits
FROM
  bank_transactions
group by Transaction_type
having  Transaction_Date BETWEEN '2024-01-01'
  AND '2024-12-31'
 limit 1

Track Orders for Post-Festival Sales Analysis

  • The analytics team is examining orders made after the major festival season in February 2023. They are interested in those orders where the total_price is between 500 and 1500 to understand customer spending trends post-festival.
  • Write an SQL query to retrieve the order_id, customer_id, order_date, and total_price for all orders within this price range placed after February 1st, 2023 from order_details table. Ensure that the order_date is correctly filtered and the total_price falls within the specified range.

Sample Output Table

order_id customer_id order_date total_price
... ... ... ... 
select order_id , customer_id, order_date, total_price from  order_details
where order_date between '2023-02-01' and '2023-12-31'
group by order_date
having total_price between 500 and 1500

Excellent Students in Grade A with Marks Between 80 and 90

  • The head of the academic department requires a list of students who have received Grade A and have marks between 80 and 90. The list should be sorted in descending order by marks.
  • This list will be used to honor the highest-performing students in this grade range. Write an SQL query to retrieve the student name, grade, and marks from the student table, ensuring the results are ordered by marks in descending order.

Sample Output Table

name grade marks
... ... ... 
select name, grade, marks from student
where grade='A' and marks  between 80 and 90
order by marks desc
