Outstanding Students in Grade A with Marks Between 80 and 90

  • The school's academic board needs a list of students who belong to Grade A and have marks between 80 and 90. This list should be sorted in descending order by marks.
  • The list will be used to highlight the most outstanding performers within this grade. Write an SQL query to retrieve the student name, grade, and marks from the student table, ensuring the results are ordered by marks in descending order.

Sample Output Table

name grade marks
... ... ... 
SELECT name , grade , marks 
FROM student
WHERE grade = 'A' AND marks between 80 AND 90
ORDER BY marks DESC;

Ranking Students Based on Their Preferred Majors

Write an SQL query to retrieve the number of students in each major program, and rank the majors based on how many students have chosen each. The most preferred major should be ranked first.

Sample Output Table:

MAJOR number_of_students
... ... 
SELECT MAJOR,
COUNT(*) AS number_of_students
FROM students_one 
GROUP BY MAJOR
ORDER BY number_of_students DESC;

Total Deposit Amount in 2024 for Budget Planning

The budgeting team needs to determine the total deposit amount for 2024 to set financial targets for the upcoming year. Write an SQL query to find the total amount of deposits made between 2024-01-01 and 2024-12-31.

Sample Output Table:

total_deposits
... 
SELECT SUM(Amount) AS total_deposits
FROM Bank_Transactions
WHERE Transaction_Type = "deposit"
AND Transaction_Date >= '2024-01-01' AND Transaction_Date <= '2024-12-31';

Sales Orders after New Year 2023

  • The sales team is reviewing orders placed after the New Year in 2023. They are particularly interested in those where the total_price is between 500 and 1500 to assess the sales performance during the early part of the year.
  • Write an SQL query to retrieve the order_id, customer_id, order_date, and total_price for all orders within this price range placed after February 1st, 2023 from order_details table. Ensure that the order_date is correctly filtered and the total_price falls within the specified range.

Sample Output Table

order_id customer_id order_date total_price
... ... ... ... 
SELECT order_id , customer_id, order_date, total_price
FROM order_details
WHERE order_date > '2023-02-01'
AND total_price BETWEEN 500 AND 1500;
