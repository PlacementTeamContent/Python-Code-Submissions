Deposits for Financial Year 2024

The finance department needs to review the total deposits made in the year 2024 for annual accounting purposes. Write an SQL query to calculate the total amount of deposits made between 2024-01-01 and 2024-12-31.

Sample Output Table:

total_deposits
... 
select sum(amount) as total_deposits 
from bank_transactions 
where 
transaction_type="deposit" and transaction_date between "2024-01-01" and "2024-12-31";

Ranking Students by Popular Majors

Write an SQL query to retrieve the total number of students enrolled in each major. Rank the majors based on the number of students, with the most popular major (highest number of students) ranked first.

Sample Output Table:

MAJOR number_of_students
... ... 
select major, count(student_id) as number_of_students
from students_one 
group by major 
order by number_of_students desc

Identify Orders for Product Launch Analysis

  • The marketing team is analyzing customer orders placed after the launch of a new product on 2023-02-01. They want to focus on orders where the total_price is between 500 and 1500 to evaluate the product's early sales.
  • Write an SQL query to retrieve the order_id, customer_id, order_date, and total_price for all orders within this price range placed after February 1st, 2023 from order_details table. Ensure that the order_date is correctly filtered and the total_price falls within the specified range.

Sample Output Table

order_id customer_id order_date total_price
... ... ... ... 
select order_id, customer_id, order_date, total_price
from order_details 
where 
total_price between 500 and 1500 and order_date>"2023-02-01";

Top Students in Grade A with Marks Between 80 and 90

  • The academic team needs a list of students who are in Grade A and have marks between 80 and 90. Additionally, they need this list sorted in descending order by marks.
  • This list will help in identifying students who are excelling within this grade range. Write an SQL query to retrieve the student name, grade, and marks for these students from the student table, ensuring the results are ordered by marks in descending order.

Sample Output Table

name grade marks
... ... ... 
select name, grade,marks 
from student 
where 
grade="A" and marks between 80 and 90 
order by 
marks desc
